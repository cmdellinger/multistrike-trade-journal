Multistrike Trade Journal Version: Toy


This is the toy version.

This is a trading journal for equities, foreign exchange, and cryptocurrencies. The goal is to track trade ideas and their outcomes. A trade idea is an instance of the execution of a trade setup, allowing multiple attempts with multiple entries and exits per attempt.

The Dashboard hasn't been implemented yet. It will have cards with summaries and graphs of trade ideas outcomes. The page is there, but it is currently just empyt frames.

This is being built using Angular and Angular Material, which is new to me, so it's a taking a bit longer than expected.
